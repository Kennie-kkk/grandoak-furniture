Our Story

Founded with a passion for creativity and comfort, Grand Oak Interior was born to make elegant, high-quality furniture accessible to every Kenyan home. We blend timeless craftsmanship with sustainable materials to produce furniture that lasts for generations.

Whether you’re furnishing a new home or refreshing your space, we’re here to bring warmth, elegance, and a personal touch to every design.
